ABCD3

ABCD3 is a dietary supplement contains vitamins, used to treat calcium and vitamin D deficiencies, nerve pain and diabetic neuropathy. It helps to build immunity, regain energy and restore the strength of muscles and bones.

Abetin-NT 100/200/400

Abetin-NT Tablets are used to treat chronic pain caused by nerve damage due to diabetes, shingles or spinal cord injury. It reduces pain and tiredness.

Deual-D

Deual-D is a used to treat gastro-esophageal reflux (acid reflux) and peptic ulcer by relieving heartburn, stomach pain, or irritation. It also neutralizes the acid and promotes easy passage of gas to reduce stomach discomfort.

Deual-L

Deual-L is used in the treatment of gastro-esophageal reflux (acid reflux), intestinal ulcers, and IBS. It reduces excess acid production in the stomach and prevents the reflux of stomach acid into the food pipe.

Dixoflam-T

Dixoflam-T is used in the treatment of pain due to muscle spasms. It improves the movement of muscles and provides relief from pain and discomfort associated with muscle spasms.

Elpihep 150/300/Susp.

Elpihep tablets and suspension are used to prevent and dissolve gallstones. It is also used to treat  primary biliary cirrhosis (PBC).

Rosidex 10/20

Rosidex 10/20 is used to lower bad cholesterol (LDL and triglycerides) and prevent heart disease, including heart attacks and strokes.

Rosidex Gold

Rosidex Gold is used for prevention of heart attack. It prevents clot formation in the blood vessels and helps to reduce increased cholesterol and triglycerides levels.

Sprezza

Sprezza reduces pain and swelling in the joints and treats RA. It also helps prevent stroke and protects the skin.

Veridiz

Veridiz tablets are used to treat vertigo are a combination medicine which acts by relaxing vascular smooth muscles of the blood vessels of the ear and improves the blood circulation. It also blocks the action of a chemical messenger that is responsible for lightheadedness in vertigo.
